Painting

These are some of the most famous paintings ever created. It is a personal selection of paintings that have a universal appeal.

The Scream by Edvard Munch

The Scream is one of the most famous paintings in the world, created by the artist Edvard Munk in 1893, symbolizing a person who is scared, screaming, and is kept at the National Gallery in Oslo, Norway.

The Last Supper by Leonardo da Vinci

The Last Supper is located in the monastery of Santa Maria delle Grazie in Milan. It is one of the most famous paintings in the world. It was painted by the Italian artist Leonardo da Vinci, as it depicts the last supper of Christ and his disciples. Designed between 1495 and 1498

The Girl With a Pearl Earring by Johannes Vermeer

Designed in the 17th century by the Dutch painter Johannes Vermeer, it is one of the most famous oil paintings in the world.

Le Moulin de la Galette by Pierre-Auguste Renoir

n 1876 painting by French artist Pierre-Auguste Renoir is on display at the Musée d'Orsay in Paris. The painting is considered more festive impressionist paintings. It depicts a gathering in the district of Moumaghtar, north of Paris.

Star Night by Vincent van Gogh

Star Night is one of the most famous paintings in the world, designed by the Dutch artist Vincent van Gogh, in 1889, and it is one of the paintings on canvas, it is worth mentioning that this painting is displayed in the Museum of Modern Art in New York City It is one of the best paintings by Van Gogh. It depicts the city of San Remy before sunrise..

Poppies in a Field by Claude Monet
